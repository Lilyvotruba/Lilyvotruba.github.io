About Me

Educational Overview

Hello, my name is Lily Votruba. I am currently a second-year student at the University of Missouri pursuing a degree in Biological Sciences with an emphasis in Medical Science and Human Biology. I recently obtained my certification as a Nursing Assistant (CNA), working to gain hands-on experience through patient care to further my career. My long-term goal is to attend Certified Anesthesiologist Assistant school after graduation to provide patients with direct anesthesia care during medical and surgical procedures, ensuring patient safety and pain management. Outside of the classroom I am involved in a Greek-letter organization. This pushes me to stay engaged with my campus community as well as provide me with resources to enhance my leadership skills. I also enjoy spending time outside through long walks while listening to music. I am very passionate about my goals, and I look forward to making a meaningful impact in my future field.
